- VOICE DELAY: Your words appear as subtitles in the air before you speak them.
- LOD ERROR: People farther than 5 feet away become low-poly, faceless shapes.
- CLIP-THRU: You momentarily sink ankle-deep into the solid floor.
- GHOST INPUT: Your hand moves to do something your mind did not command.
- SOUND LEAK: You hear the background music of the simulation as loud white noise.
- SENSORY SWAP: You smell the color blue or see the sound of the bus.
- TIME JUMP: You teleport 10 feet forward with no memory of the steps.
- ASSET FLIP: A common object is replaced by a strange item.
- THE VOID-EYE: For a split second, everyone's eyes turn into black pits.
- SKY-BOX TEAR: Looking up reveals the sky is a flat, flickering image file.
