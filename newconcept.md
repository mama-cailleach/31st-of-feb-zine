GAME SYSTEM SUMMARY
Core Concept
A solo RPG played through an e‑zine and an online terminal‑style companion app.
The player is trapped in a repeating day loop.
The day is “normal,” but includes a mandatory job interview.
To break the loop, the player must reduce the day’s Success Probability to 0%, which is mathematically unreachable — ensuring the loop continues.

CHARACTER SHEET
Attributes

- SNS — Sensory
Processing noise, movement, overstimulation, and shifting focus.

MECHANICS
Probability System (d100‑based)
- Each event in the day has a probability expressed as a decimal (e.g., 0.25).
- The day’s total probability = product of all event probabilities.
- Example:
0.25\times 0.13=0.0325- The player aims to reach 0%, which is impossible.
- Therefore, the loop repeats.
